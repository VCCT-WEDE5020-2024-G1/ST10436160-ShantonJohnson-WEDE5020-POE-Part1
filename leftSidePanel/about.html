Story & People

The Green Earth Initiative (GEI) began as a grassroots effort to combat climate change and environmental degradation. Since then, we have grown into a nationwide movement, empowering over 50,000 individuals to take action and planting 1 million trees through our hands-on programs. Our diverse team of 100+ environmental experts and dedicated volunteers are united by a shared mission: to protect the planet and create a sustainable future for all.

Mission & Impact

Faced with critical environmental threats—like the fact that 80% of natural habitats are at risk—GEI takes direct action through community engagement and education. We have helped reduce local carbon emissions by 30%, diverted 100,000 tons of waste from landfills, and advocated for policies that make sustainability accessible to everyone. By collaborating with 200+ businesses, local governments, and other NGOs, we foster collective efforts to fight climate change and make eco-friendly practices part of everyday life.

Looking Forward

GEI's future is bright as we continue to expand our initiatives. In the next five years, we plan to create 20 new urban green spaces and introduce renewable energy solutions to power 10,000 homes sustainably. We are committed to long-term growth, and by 2049, we aim to be a global leader in environmental advocacy, driving innovation and fostering a culture of sustainability in every corner of the world.
